ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE


What exactly is artificial intelligence?

Artificial intelligence is comprised of two words.

Artificial means “man-made”,
and intelligence refers to “thinking power”.


Therefore, AI defines “a man-made thinking power”. .


Alan Turing once defined artificial intelligence as
“the science of making machines do things that would require intelligence if done by man.”


In simple words, the science and engineering of making intelligent machines like computer programmers is what AI stands for.
